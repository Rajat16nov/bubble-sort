## ChatGPT Prompts for Company Research

### Identify the company's core products or services

ChatGPT can be used to identify a company's core products or services using the information available online.

You can feed it with details from the company's website, press releases, news articles, or any other reliable sources.

ChatGPT can analyze this data to provide a summary of the company's main offerings and services.

For example, you can ask ChatGPT to identify the core products or services of a tech company.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a business analyst and identify the core products or services of this company.

Here are some details from their website, press releases, and news articles:
```

### Gather company's financial performance data

Analyzing a company's financial performance can be complex, but with ChatGPT, it becomes straightforward.

Provide ChatGPT with the name of a company and specify the financial data you need - such as net income, revenue, liabilities, or cash flow.

ChatGPT can then present this information in a more simplified and understandable format.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Focus on net income, revenue, total liabilities, and cash flow for the last 5 fiscal years.

Please present the data in an easy-to-understand format.
```

### Conduct SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)

ChatGPT can be a handy tool to conduct a SWOT analysis of a company based on public data.

You can feed ChatGPT with company's financial reports, market reputation, competitor analysis and other relevant information, and ask it to analyze for strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.

It can analyze various factors like financial health, market position, competition, and growth prospects among others.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Conduct a SWOT analysis for Apple Inc.

based on the following information:

Financial Health: Strong with large cash reserves,

Market Position: Leader in the smartphone and wearable market,

Competition: Increasing competition in smartphone and service segments,

Growth Prospects: Promising, with increasing focus on services and wearables.
```
