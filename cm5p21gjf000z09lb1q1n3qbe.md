### Discuss different types of financial markets

Financial markets are complex ecosystems where individuals, companies, and governments trade financial securities.

There are broadly four types: capital markets, money markets, derivative markets, and forex markets.

Capital markets deal with long-term investments like stocks and bonds.

Money markets are for short-term borrowing and lending.

Derivative markets trade in derivatives such as futures and options.

Forex markets enable currency exchange.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a financial advisor and explain the differences between capital markets, money markets, derivative markets, and forex markets.

Provide an overview of each type and their significant roles in the financial ecosystem.
```
