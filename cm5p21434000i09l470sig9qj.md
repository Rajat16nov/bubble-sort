### Develop a hypothesis and design an experiment

The hypothesis could be: Students who do their homework regularly achieve better grades than those who don't.

To design an experiment, divide a class into two groups.

One group will complete their homework consistently, while the other group will not be assigned any homework.

After a specified period, compare the academic performance of the two groups.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Design an experiment to test this hypothesis, outlining the control and experimental groups, the independent and dependent variables, and the process to collect and analyze the data.
```
