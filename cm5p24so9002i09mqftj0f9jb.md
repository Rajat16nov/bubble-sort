## ChatGPT Prompts for Resume From Job Description

### Identify key skills required from the job description

With the help of ChatGPT, you can easily identify the essential skills required for a particular job.

Just provide the job description to ChatGPT, and it will pinpoint the key skills mentioned in the description.

This can help you tailor your resume to the job, increasing your chances of being considered for the position.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

The job is for a marketing manager, and here is the description:
```

### Translate job description requirements into resume bullet points

If you're struggling to tailor your resume to a specific job description, ChatGPT can help translate those requirements into concise and impactful resume bullet points.

All you have to do is provide the job description and your current resume details, and ChatGPT will show you how to highlight your relevant skills and experiences.

For instance, you can ask ChatGPT to match the requirements of a job description with your skills and responsibilities from previous roles.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```
