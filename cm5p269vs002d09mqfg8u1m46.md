### Identify ideal image size and aspect ratio for Instagram posts

Instagram has specific preferences for the size and aspect ratio of images for optimal display.

The ideal Instagram post size is 1080px by 1080px with an aspect ratio of 1:1 for a perfect square post.

For portrait posts, use an aspect ratio of 4:5 with a size of 1080px by 1350px, and for landscape posts, use an aspect ratio of 1.91:1 with a size of 1080px by 566px.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As a social media manager, identify and explain the ideal image size and aspect ratio for Instagram posts.

Also, provide any extra tips and considerations for optimizing Instagram photo posts.
```
