### Identify project goals and objectives

Project management is all about defining and achieving feasible objectives.

With ChatGPT, you can clarify your project goals and objectives, which will help you align your team and set clear expectations.

Provide the AI with information about your project, such as its purpose, the expected outcomes, and the resources available.

ChatGPT can then guide you in identifying key objectives and goals for the project to be successful.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

The project is to develop a mobile app for online shopping with a tight budget and deadline.

Help me identify the key goals and objectives for this project.
```
