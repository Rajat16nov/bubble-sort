## ChatGPT Prompts for Teachers

### Prepare a lesson plan

ChatGPT can assist you in preparing a structured lesson plan for your teaching sessions.

Provide it with the subject, grade, and the specific topic you plan to teach, and it will come up with a well-structured, comprehensive lesson plan.

Remember, lesson plans prepared by ChatGPT will include learning objectives, activities, and evaluation methods.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As a seasoned educator, I need your assistance in preparing a lesson plan.

The subject is English, for a 7th-grade class, and the topic of the lesson is 'Introduction to Poetry'.

Please prepare a comprehensive lesson plan including learning objectives, activities and assessment methods.
```
