## ChatGPT Prompts for Literature Review

### Identify a research question or thesis statement

The process of identifying a research question or thesis statement can be daunting.

However, ChatGPT can aid you in formulating a precise and research-worthy statement based on your given literature.

Simply provide the AI with a detailed overview of the literature you've read, and it will assist in creating a compelling research question or thesis statement.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Based on the following literature overview, help me identify a research question or thesis statement.

Here's a summary of the literature I've studied:
```

### Conduct an initial literature search

Initiating a literature search is the first major step in a literature review, and ChatGPT can assist in this process.

Feed ChatGPT with your research topic, and ask it to find related scholarly articles, studies, books, or any other significant academic sources.

ChatGPT can provide a brief summary of these resources, helping you to understand the existing knowledge on your topic.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a research assistant and conduct an initial literature search on the topic: 'The impact of climate change on agricultural productivity'.

Find relevant academic sources and provide a brief summary of each.
```

### Perform a thorough literature review

ChatGPT can assist researchers and students in performing a thorough literature review, a process that traditionally consumes a lot of time and effort.

You can feed it with your research objectives or questions, and it can go through related articles, books, and other sources, extracting critical points and summarizing the findings.

Furthermore, ChatGPT can help in identifying gaps in the existing literature and suggesting areas for future research.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Perform an in-depth literature review on the topic of Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare.

Summarize the main findings, identify gaps in the current literature, and suggest potential areas for future research.
```

### Define the scope of the review

With ChatGPT, crafting a well-structured and accurate literature review becomes significantly easier.

You can leverage ChatGPT to define the scope of your review by providing a broad research area or a specific research question.

ChatGPT can aid in determining the relevant time frame, research methods, geographical region, or specific theories that your literature review should cover.

For example, you can ask ChatGPT to list the main areas to focus on while reviewing literature on a specific topic.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```

Using ChatGPT, you can feed it with abstracts or summaries of existing research and ask it to identify potential areas of unexplored or under-researched topics.

For instance, you can ask ChatGPT to highlight areas that lack consensus, subjects that have not been studied in depth, or questions that were left unanswered in previous research.

### Properly cite all sources in your chosen style

Citing sources correctly is fundamental to a comprehensive literature review.

ChatGPT can assist you in creating accurate citations in your chosen citation style.

All you need to do is provide the necessary information about the source, such as author(s), publication date, title, and publisher details, and ChatGPT will format the citation accordingly.

For instance, you can ask ChatGPT to cite a book in APA style.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Please create a citation for the following book in APA style:

Author(s): John Doe

Year of Publication: 2020

Title: AI and Future Society

Publisher: Tech Books Publishing

City of publication: New York
```
