## ChatGPT Prompts for SEO

### Perform local SEO analysis

ChatGPT can assist with local SEO analysis by examining the performance of specific keywords in your area, analyzing local competitor strategies, and evaluating your website's local search engine results page (SERP) ranking.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Here are the website details:
```

ChatGPT can assist you in implementing your keyword mapping strategy, identifying potential keywords related to your content, and planning how to use them effectively.

Inform ChatGPT of your website status and ask it to guide you in securing your website with HTTPS.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```
