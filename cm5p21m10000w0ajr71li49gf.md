### Conduct competitor product analysis for ecommerce products

Gaining insights into your competitors' strategies, product offerings, and customer reviews can help your ecommerce business stay ahead.

You can use ChatGPT to conduct a detailed analysis of your competitors' products.

Provide the AI with details of your competitor's product and ask it to compare the features, pricing, and customer feedback with your own product.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Our product is Product A and our competitor's product is Product B.

Analyze the features, pricing, customer reviews and overall market positioning of both products.
```
