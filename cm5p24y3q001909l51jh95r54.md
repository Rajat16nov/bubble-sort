## ChatGPT Prompts for Resume Making

### Identify relevant professional skills

Every profession requires a unique set of skills.

For instance, a software developer needs coding skills, while a project manager needs leadership skills.

ChatGPT can help identify these professional skills based on the career or job role you specify.

This can be beneficial when crafting a resume, as it will give you an idea of what skills to highlight.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an expert in resume making, identify the relevant professional skills for a career in digital marketing.
```

### List past work experience in reverse chronological order

ChatGPT can help you organize your work experiences in your resume, starting with the most recent job and going back in time.

You just need to provide the details of your previous roles, including the company name, role title, and the period you worked there.

This can help in maintaining a clear, concise, and professional resume that is easy to read for potential employers.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a professional resume writer and organize the following work experiences in reverse chronological order:

1.

Junior Developer at XYZ Tech from 2015-2017

2.

Senior Developer at ABC Corp from 2017-2019

3.

Lead Developer at 123 Solutions from 2019-Present
```
