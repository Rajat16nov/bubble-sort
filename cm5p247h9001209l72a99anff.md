Business consulting operates on a problem-solving model where consultants analyze the client's business and provide solutions to improve efficiency and profitability.

ChatGPT can break down various aspects of a business model, such as value proposition, customer segments, revenue streams, and more.

For instance, you can ask it to assess the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats (SWOT analysis) of your current business model.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Conduct a SWOT analysis and provide recommendations to improve profitability and growth.

Here is the information about the company:
```
