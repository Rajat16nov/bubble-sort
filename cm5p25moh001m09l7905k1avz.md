### Share tips on how to make a cake rise evenly

To ensure your cake rises evenly, always preheat your oven before baking and place the batter in the center of the oven.

Use fresh ingredients, as stale baking powder or soda can prevent a cake from rising.

Distribute the batter evenly in the cake pan, and avoid opening the oven door during baking as the temperature drop can cause the cake to collapse.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
You are an experienced baker.

Share some essential tips and common mistakes to avoid when it comes to getting a cake to rise evenly during baking.
```
