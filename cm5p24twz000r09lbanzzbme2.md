### Understand the purpose of a professional summary

The purpose of a professional summary in a resume is to provide a succinct overview of your skills, experiences, and achievements that are relevant to the job you're applying for.

It's the first section that hiring managers usually read, so it should capture their attention and convince them that you're a qualified and suitable candidate for the job.

You can use ChatGPT to help you write a compelling professional summary by providing it with your key skills and experiences.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Given that I have 5 years of experience in project management, proficient in using project management software, and have a track record of completing projects within the set deadlines, act as a resume writer and help me write a professional summary for a project manager role.
```
