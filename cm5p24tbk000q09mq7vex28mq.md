## ChatGPT Prompts for Resume Tailoring

### Identify and list relevant skills for the target job

ChatGPT can be invaluable for job seekers looking to tailor their resumes to specific positions.

Input a job description, and ChatGPT can help identify the necessary skills and experiences required for the role.

It can also provide assistance by highlighting which of these skills should be emphasized on your resume.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a career consultant to help tailor my resume.

Here's the job description for a role I'm applying for.

Identify the key skills required and advise on which ones I should emphasize on my resume.
```

With the job description at hand, ChatGPT can help you tailor your resume by matching your skills and experiences with the job requirements.

Let the AI know about your skill set, past experiences and the job you're applying for, and it will create a customized resume summary for you.

ChatGPT can also suggest areas of improvement or additional skills you may need to acquire.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Here is my set of skills and past work experience:
```
