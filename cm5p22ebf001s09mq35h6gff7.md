### Understand the concept of fixed, cardinal, and mutable signs

Astrology categorizes the twelve zodiac signs into fixed, cardinal, and mutable signs.

Each group has its unique characteristics.

Cardinal signs (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn) are initiators.

They are associated with new beginnings and start of the seasons.

Fixed signs (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius) are the stabilizers, known for their determination and resistance to change.

Mutable signs (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces) are the adaptors of the zodiac, symbolizing change, flexibility, and the end of seasons.
