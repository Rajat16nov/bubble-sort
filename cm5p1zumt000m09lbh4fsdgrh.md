## ChatGPT Prompts for News Writing

### Generate ideas for news stories

In the fast-paced world of news, coming up with fresh and engaging story ideas can be a challenge.

But, ChatGPT can help you identify potential story angles, hot topics, or overlooked subjects in your area of interest.

Simply provide a general news category or theme, and ChatGPT can generate newsworthy story ideas.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced news editor to generate compelling news story ideas.

The focus is on technology advancements and their impact on society.

Give me 5 interesting news story ideas that I could assign to my journalists.
```

### Conduct interviews with relevant sources

ChatGPT can simulate conducting interviews, gathering information, and creating insights, making it a valuable tool for news writing.

You can ask ChatGPT to role-play as a journalist interviewing a source about a specific event, topic, or issue.

However, keep in mind that it can only generate responses based on its training data and can't interact with real people.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Role-play as an investigative journalist interviewing a whistleblower about recent corporate malpractices.

Ask questions to gather as much detailed and specific information as possible.

Start the interview with the following question: Can you share details about the malpractice you witnessed in the corporation?
```

### Write a compelling headline

Crafting a catchy headline can make or break your news article.

Grabbing your reader's attention is key, and ChatGPT can assist you in this task.

Give it the context of the news article, and it can generate compelling headlines that will entice readers to click and read further.

For instance, you can feed it with a short summary of your news story and ask for a headline.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced news editor and create a compelling headline for the following news story:

Local mayor announces a groundbreaking initiative to combat homelessness in the city by providing job training and affordable housing options.
```

### Develop an engaging lede

Crafting a compelling lede is the heart of news writing, and with ChatGPT's help, it's never been easier.

Provide ChatGPT with key details of the news story - who, what, where, when, why, and how - and it can help you frame an engaging lede that instantly grabs your reader's attention.

For instance, you can ask ChatGPT to generate a lede for a breaking news story.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced news writer and develop an engaging lede for the following news story:

A local tech company has just launched a groundbreaking AI software that can predict weather patterns with 98% accuracy.

The software was unveiled today at their headquarters.
```

### Structure the news story (Inverted Pyramid)

When it comes to news writing, ChatGPT can help structure your story in the Inverted Pyramid format, which is the standard in journalism.

The inverted pyramid structure prioritizes information from most to least important, ensuring that the reader gets the most critical details upfront.

Just provide the facts of the story and ask ChatGPT to structure it accordingly.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced journalist and structure the following information in the Inverted Pyramid format.

Key facts: A fire broke out in a downtown office building yesterday evening, leading to the evacuation of hundreds of workers.

Firefighters were able to contain the blaze after two hours.

No casualties were reported, but damage to the building is extensive.
```
