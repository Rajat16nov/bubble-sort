## ChatGPT Prompts for Resume Improvement

### Identify key skills for the job application

ChatGPT can assist you in identifying the key skills needed for your job application.

ChatGPT can help you tailor your resume to match the job requirements, increasing your chances of landing an interview.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```

### Advise on the appropriate resume type based on career stage

Resumes come in many shapes and sizes, and the type you should use can depend on where you are in your career.

For instance, new graduates might benefit from a functional resume focusing on skills over experience, while professionals with a long work history might need a chronological resume to highlight their career progression.

ChatGPT can provide personalized recommendations based on your career stage and goals.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an experienced career counselor, advise on the appropriate resume type considering my career stage and goals.

I have recently graduated with a degree in Computer Science, and I'm looking to start my career in software development.
```

### Suggest strategies to handle employment gaps in the resume

A resume with employment gaps doesn't have to be a deal-breaker.

ChatGPT can suggest strategies like focusing on transferable skills, considering a functional resume format, or explaining gaps honestly yet positively.

You can also ask it to recommend ways to fill gaps with productive activities such as freelance work, volunteering, or upskilling.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

