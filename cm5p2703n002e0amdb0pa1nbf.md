## ChatGPT Prompts for Annual Review

### Set specific and measurable goals for the upcoming year

Utilize ChatGPT to help set clear and quantifiable goals for the upcoming year based on your last annual review.

It can assist in identifying areas for improvement and setting objectives for each department.

For instance, you can ask ChatGPT to suggest ways to increase productivity by 20% or cut costs by 15%.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a strategic planner and based on the last annual review, suggest five specific and measurable goals that our organization should aim to achieve in the upcoming year to improve overall performance.

Here is the summary of our last year's review:
```

### Review achievements and challenges of the past year

ChatGPT can assist in reviewing an organization's performance, achievements, and challenges from the past year, summarizing the key aspects effectively.

This can be anything from significant milestones achieved, areas of improvement identified, or challenges encountered and how they were addressed.

Just provide ChatGPT with the necessary data or summarize the year's activities, and it will break it down for you.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an experienced business analyst, review the achievements and challenges of our organization in the past year.

Here is a summary of our year:
```

For an annual review, ChatGPT can help identify areas of improvement and growth based on the detailed analysis of previous performance data, feedbacks, or any related documents.
