## ChatGPT Prompts for Meeting Notes

### Identify the meeting's purpose and objectives

ChatGPT can assist in identifying the purpose and objectives of a meeting by analyzing provided meeting notes.

It can highlight the key points, decisions made, tasks assigned, and the overall outcome of the meeting.

For example, you can feed ChatGPT with the minutes of the meeting and ask it to determine the main objectives and outcomes.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As a professional minute analyst, examine these meeting notes and identify the main purpose and objectives of the meeting.

Here are the meeting notes for your review:
```

### List the participants

ChatGPT can assist in documenting the participants of a meeting, including their roles and responsibilities.

It can also keep track of who attended and who was absent.

You can feed it with the participants' names and ask it to recall their attendance in the future.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As a meeting notes assistant, list down the participants of our monthly sales meeting.

Indicate their roles and responsibilities and keep a record of their attendance.

Here are the participants:
```

### Note the meeting start and end time

ChatGPT can be a helpful tool in recording the precise start and end times of your meetings, making it easier for you to keep track of the meeting's duration.

You simply need to feed the meeting's start and end times into ChatGPT, and it will note them down accurately.

This feature can be particularly useful for those who need to log or bill their hours.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a diligent secretary who records the start and end times of a meeting.

The meeting started at 9:00 AM and ended at 10:30 AM.

Note these times accurately.
```

### Summarize key discussion points

Use ChatGPT to summarize the important discussion points from your meetings.

By feeding it your meeting minutes or notes, it can identify the main issues discussed, decisions made, action items, and the responsible parties.

ChatGPT can also help in extracting any deadlines mentioned or follow-up steps required, helping in better meeting management and increased productivity.

For example, ask ChatGPT to summarize the key points from a set of detailed meeting notes.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```

### Identify the decisions made during the meeting

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

