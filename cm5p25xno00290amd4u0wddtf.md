### Understand the concept of compound interest

Compound interest plays a significant role in money management as it multiplies your money over time.

When you invest or save, compound interest is calculated on the initial principal and also on the accumulated interest of previous periods.

This means that your money grows at an increasing rate.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Help me understand how it works, its benefits, and how it can positively impact my personal finance management in the long run.
```

### Understand the concept of net worth and how to increase it

Net worth is the total value of your assets minus your liabilities.

It's a key measure of your financial health, and increasing it should be a primary goal.

Improving net worth involves reducing liabilities and increasing assets.

This could be achieved by saving more, investing wisely, reducing debt, or increasing income sources.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```
