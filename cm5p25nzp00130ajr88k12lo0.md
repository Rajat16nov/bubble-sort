### Design distractors for multiple-choice questions

Designing effective distractors for multiple-choice questions can be a challenge.

Distractors should be plausible but incorrect, forcing the test-taker to truly understand the material.

ChatGPT can help you generate convincing distractors for your multiple-choice questions based on the correct answer and the context.

Just provide ChatGPT with the correct answer and a brief context, and it will come up with plausible yet incorrect distractors.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

The topic is American History.

The question is: Who was the first president of the United States?

The correct answer is George Washington.

Generate 3 plausible but incorrect distractors.
```
