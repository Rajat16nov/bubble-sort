## ChatGPT Prompts for Horror Stories

### Generate a chilling horror story title

Whether you're an author struggling with writer's block or simply need a spine-chilling title for a horror story, ChatGPT can help you out.

Just provide the AI with the premise of your horror story, and it will create an eerie and suspenseful title that attracts readers.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

The story revolves around a group of teenagers who discover an old, haunted mansion in their small town.

What should I call it?
```
