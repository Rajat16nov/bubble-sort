## ChatGPT Prompts for Journalists

### Research on a specific topic or person

Harness the power of ChatGPT to conduct in-depth research on a specific topic or person.

You can feed the model with the name of a person or a topic related to journalism, and it will provide you with comprehensive information.

The AI can accumulate data from various resources and deliver synthesized summaries, making research more efficient and faster.

For example, you could ask ChatGPT to provide information on the role of investigative journalism in a democratic society.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced journalist and provide a detailed analysis of the role and impact of investigative journalism in a democratic society.

Draw from historical examples and recent events.
```

```plaintext

```

### Identify potential sources for a story

As a journalist, finding reliable sources for your story can be difficult.

But, ChatGPT can help in identifying potential sources.

Provide a brief on your story to the AI model, and it can provide a list of potential sources such as experts in the field, organizations, documents, or databases.

For instance, if your story is about climate change, ChatGPT can suggest environmental scientists, research reports, or NGOs to reach out to.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```
