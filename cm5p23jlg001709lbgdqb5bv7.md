## ChatGPT Prompts for Improving Academic Writing

### Identify the purpose of your academic writing

Understanding the purpose of your academic writing is crucial to convey your ideas effectively and persuasively.

ChatGPT can help you identify this purpose by examining the requirements of your academic task or assignment, as well as your intended audience.

Whether it's to inform, persuade, describe or narrate, ChatGPT can guide you in creating an academic writing piece that fulfills its intended purpose.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

I have a task to write a research paper on the topic of 'The Impact of Climate Change on Global Economy'.

What should be the main purpose of my writing in this context?
```
