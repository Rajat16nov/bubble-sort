## ChatGPT Prompts for Tone of Voice

### Define your brand's tone of voice

Whether you're starting a new venture or redefining an existing brand, ChatGPT can assist in defining your brand's tone of voice.

ChatGPT can consider your target audience, brand values, and your desired impression to create a distinct tone of voice.
