### Calculate capitalization rate and cash-on-cash return

Provide ChatGPT with your property’s net operating income and current market value, and it can help calculate the capitalization rate.

Similarly, if you have the cash flow before tax and the total cash investment, ChatGPT can help compute the cash-on-cash return.

Both these measures are essential for real estate investors to determine the profitability and potential return on a property investment.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a skilled real estate analyst and help calculate the capitalization rate and the cash-on-cash return for a property.

The property's net operating income is $50,000, and the current market value is $700,000.

The cash flow before tax is $4,000 and the total cash investment was $60,000.
```

### Identify the benefits of real estate investment trusts (REITs)

REITs offer a unique avenue for real estate investing, particularly for those who wish to diversify their portfolio.

They provide a way to invest in real estate without the need to directly own property.

REITs typically offer higher dividend yields compared to other types of investments.

Moreover, they are highly liquid, unlike physical real estate, which can take time to buy and sell.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Also, compare them with traditional real estate investments in terms of liquidity, dividends, and portfolio diversification.
```
