### Choose a genre for reading

If you are indecisive about what genre to read next, ChatGPT can help you make a choice.

Just provide ChatGPT with a brief description of the kind of stories or books you enjoy, and it will recommend a genre for you.

Whether you like adventurous tales, romantic novels, or insightful biographies, ChatGPT can guide you towards your next great read.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

I enjoy books that take me on an emotional journey, filled with complex relationships and character growth.

What genre should I explore next?
```
