## ChatGPT Prompts for Writing CV

### Identify essential personal details to include

When writing a CV, it's crucial to include certain personal details that employers look for.

ChatGPT can help you identify these essential details, which usually include your full name, contact information, professional summary, work experience, education, and skills.

You may also want to add certifications, awards, or volunteer work if relevant.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

My background is in software engineering, with 5 years of experience.
```
