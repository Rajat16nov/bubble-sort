## ChatGPT Prompts for Assignments

### Understand the assignment brief

Whether it's an academic paper or a corporate project, understanding the assignment brief is essential.

Turn to ChatGPT for help.

It can dissect the brief, identify the key points, and give an overview of what needs to be done.

For instance, you can feed an assignment brief to ChatGPT and ask it to summarize the key tasks or objectives.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a study partner helping to understand the assignment brief.

Here's the brief for an upcoming assignment:
```

### Identify key points to address in the assignment

ChatGPT can aid you in identifying the key points that need to be addressed in your assignment.

It can help you break down the assignment into manageable parts and prioritize the areas that need to be focused on.

All you have to do is provide the guidelines or a brief of the assignment to the AI.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```

### Conduct preliminary research on the assignment topic

ChatGPT can help you conduct preliminary research for your assignment topic.

Just provide the topic and specific questions you need to address or aspects you need to understand.

ChatGPT can provide a brief summary, list of key points, or relevant insights based on your needs.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```
