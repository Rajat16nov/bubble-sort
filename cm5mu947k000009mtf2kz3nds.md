## ChatGPT Prompts for Resume and Cover Letter

### Identify key skills and experiences for your resume

ChatGPT can help you identify key skills and experiences that are essential for your resume and cover letter.

To do this, you'll need to provide some information about the job or industry you're targeting.

The AI will then generate a list of skills and experiences that are often sought after in that field.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

I am aiming for a managerial position in the software development industry.

What skills and experiences should I highlight?
```
