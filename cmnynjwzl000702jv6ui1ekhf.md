### 💰 OpenAI Acquihires a Fintech Startup That's Good at Math

### 🚗 Nissan Cuts Models, Bets Everything on AI Driving

* Nissan is [slashing its global lineup from 56 to 45 models](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/nissan-streamline-global-lineup-45-models-56-2026-04-14/?utm_source=galaxy.ai&utm_medium=newsletter) and plans to deploy AI driving tech in **90% of its vehicles** long-term under CEO Ivan Espinosa's turnaround strategy. They've partnered with Uber and British startup Wayve for robotaxis, targeting a Tokyo pilot by late 2026 and end-to-end autonomous capability in its new Elgrand minivan by end of fiscal 2027.

* **Why it matters:** Nissan is making AI-powered driving a core pillar of its *survival strategy*. For a company that's been struggling for years, this is either a brilliant pivot or a very expensive way to find out it wasn't.

### 🔬 Heartflow Sues Rival Cleerly Over AI Heart Diagnostics Patents

* Heartflow (valued at $2.27B after its Nasdaq debut) [sued competitor Cleerly in Texas federal court](https://www.reuters.com/legal/litigation/heartflow-sues-rival-cleerly-over-ai-cardiology-technology-2026-04-13/?utm_source=galaxy.ai&utm_medium=newsletter), alleging a former consultant secretly used Heartflow's patented AI diagnostic tech to launch the rival company back in 2017. * **Why it matters:** The consultant-to-competitor pipeline just became a legal minefield. As AI transforms medical diagnostics, this case could set precedent for how AI health tech IP gets protected. Spicy.
