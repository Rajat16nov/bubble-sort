## ChatGPT Prompts for Writing Literature Review

### Understand the purpose of a literature review

A literature review is a comprehensive overview of scholarly works related to a specific topic or research question.

It allows researchers to understand the current state of research, identify gaps or inconsistencies, and pinpoint areas for further exploration.

In the context of thesis or dissertation, it establishes a theoretical framework and methodology for the research.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an experienced academic writer, explain the purpose and importance of a literature review in the context of a research study.

Also, identify potential strategies for conducting an effective literature review.
```

### Identify and define the research topic

ChatGPT can assist you in identifying and defining your research topic for a literature review.

To begin, simply feed it with a broad field of study or your area of interest.

ChatGPT will then propose several related research topics, each accompanied by a brief definition or problem statement.

For example, you can ask ChatGPT to generate research topics related to the impact of technology in education.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
I'm planning to write a literature review on the impact of technology in education.

Could you suggest a few focused research topics and provide a short definition for each?
```

### Conduct a comprehensive literature search

The backbone of a strong literature review is a comprehensive, focused search of existing literature on your topic.

With ChatGPT, you can find relevant sources, analyze them, and identify gaps in the current research.

Provide ChatGPT with your research question, and it will give you guidance on how to conduct a comprehensive search in academic databases.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

My research question is about the impact of social media on teenagers' mental health.

How should I approach my literature search in academic databases?
```
