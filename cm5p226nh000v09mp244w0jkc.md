### Conduct SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)

ChatGPT can simulate a SWOT analysis by examining various aspects of a business, including internal factors like resources and capabilities (strengths and weaknesses), and external factors such as industry trends and market dynamics (opportunities and threats).

To conduct this analysis, ChatGPT would need relevant information about the business, such as its current position, market competition, resources, and strategic goals.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As a business development expert, conduct a SWOT analysis for our business.

Here's some information about us:

- We are a tech startup focusing on AI solutions.

- We have a strong technical team but lack marketing expertise.

- Our main competitors are established tech companies with a broad customer base.

- The market is growing, and there's a growing demand for AI-driven solutions.
```
