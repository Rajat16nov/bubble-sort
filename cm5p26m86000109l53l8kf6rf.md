## ChatGPT Prompts for Financial Planning

### Understand your current financial situation

Understanding one's financial situation is the cornerstone of effective financial planning.

By feeding ChatGPT with your financial data such as income, expenses, assets, and liabilities, it can help in analyzing and providing a comprehensive view of your financial health.

For instance, you can ask ChatGPT to calculate the ratio of your savings to expenses, or how long your current savings would last if you lost your income source.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As a financial advisor, help me understand my current financial situation.

Here is my financial data:

Monthly income: $5000

Monthly expenses: $3000

Total savings: $20000

Total debt: $10000
```

Understanding and setting financial goals is a critical step in effective financial planning.

ChatGPT can help by guiding you through the process, including helping you identify short, medium, and long term financial objectives based on your income, expenses, and future plans.

For example, you might ask ChatGPT to suggest goals based on your current financial situation.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```
