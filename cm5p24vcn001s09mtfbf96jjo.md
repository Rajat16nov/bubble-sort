## ChatGPT Prompts for Rewriting Resume

### Analyze current resume for strengths and weaknesses

ChatGPT can be utilized to identify strengths and weaknesses in your current resume.

To use this feature, provide your resume details to ChatGPT and ask it to analyze it.

```plaintext

```

ChatGPT can help you identify and emphasize your transferable skills while rewriting your resume.

Provide a brief description of your work experience and skills, and ChatGPT can highlight those skills that are adaptable across various industries and roles.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

