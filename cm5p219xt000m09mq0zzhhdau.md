### Learn the difference between outcome goals and process goals

Outcome goals focus on the end result or achievement, such as I want to lose 20 pounds or I want to save $10,000.

They are generally not fully in our control as they depend on various factors.

On the other hand, process goals are the steps you take to achieve your outcome goals, like I will exercise for 30 minutes daily or I will save $200 every month.

These are entirely in our control and can lead to the accomplishment of the outcome goals.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As a professional life coach, explain the difference between outcome goals and process goals.

Provide examples for better understanding and how process goals can lead to the achievement of outcome goals.
```
