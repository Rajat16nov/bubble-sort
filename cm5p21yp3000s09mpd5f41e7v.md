## ChatGPT Prompts for Designers

### Explore design inspirations

Designers are always on the lookout for fresh and unique inspirations to fuel their creativity.

ChatGPT can help you explore and identify exciting design inspirations based on your preferences or specific design style.

Provide the AI with a description of the style or the type of design you're interested in, and it will generate ideas or directions for you to explore.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

I am working on a minimalist interior design project for a small urban apartment.

Please suggest some modern and innovative design ideas and inspirations.
```
