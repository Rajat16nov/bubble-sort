### 🚀 OpenAI Drops GPT-5 Like It's 2019

-   GPT-5 just landed for all 700 million ChatGPT users --- even the freeloaders get "PhD-level" AI that can supposedly code entire apps from vibes alone.

-   Why it matters: OpenAI's betting $100+ billion in compute costs that this model will finally justify the hype. No pressure or anything.

### 🤔 AI Critic Says "Not So Fast, Buckaroo"

-   Gary Marcus threw cold water on the GPT-5 party, pointing out it's still just "pretty good" at benchmarks, not the AGI messiah everyone ordered.

-   Why it matters: Someone had to be the adult in the room. Turns out making computers think is still harder than making VCs invest.

### 🏭 SoftBank Buys a Whole Factory for Its AI Dreams

-   SoftBank just purchased an entire EV plant in Ohio to fuel its $500 billion "Stargate" data center project. Because apparently building AI requires owning actual real estate now.

-   Why it matters: When you're spending half a trillion dollars on AI infrastructure, buying factories becomes a rounding error.

* * * * *

### 📈 Wall Street Loves SoftBank's Expensive Hobby

-   SoftBank's stock jumped 13% to record highs after announcing its AI spending spree. Investors are basically throwing money at anyone who says "AI" and "billions" in the same sentence.

-   Why it matters: The market has decided that burning cash on AI is the new printing money. What could go wrong?

* * * * *

### 🔌 Tesla Kills Its Dojo Supercomputer Dream

-   Elon just shut down Tesla's in-house Dojo supercomputer team, admitting that building AI chips is harder than tweeting about them.

-   Why it matters: Even Tesla can't afford to play hardware hero against Nvidia. Sometimes you just gotta buy the GPUs like everyone else.

* * * * *

### 💸 AI Coding Startups Discover Basic Economics

-   Turns out selling AI coding assistants for less than they cost to run isn't a sustainable business model. Who could have predicted this shocking development?

-   Why it matters: The AI gold rush just met the harsh reality of "gross margins." Spoiler alert: negative numbers are bad.

* * * * *

### ⚖️ Courts Start Calling BS on "AI Washing"

-   Companies are getting sued for lying about their AI capabilities. Apparently "revolutionary machine learning" doesn't cover a bunch of if-statements.

-   Why it matters: The legal system is finally catching up to marketing departments. RIP to all the "AI-powered" Excel macros.

### 📢 Grok Is About to Become a Walking Advertisement

-   Elon's adding ads directly into Grok's responses because those "expensive GPUs" won't pay for themselves. Your AI therapist is now sponsored by NordVPN.

-   Why it matters: Get ready for chatbots that sound like they're reading from a telemarketing script. "Have you tried turning it off and on again? Speaking of which, have you heard about our sponsor?"

### 🦉 Duolingo Fires Humans, Makes Bank

-   Despite Twitter outrage over replacing contractors with AI, Duolingo's revenue crushed expectations and its stock soared. Turns out users care more about free lessons than employment ethics.

-   Why it matters: The market has spoken: cheap AI beats expensive humans, even when everyone's mad about it.

* * * * *

### 💔 Bumble's AI Makeover Flops Harder Than a Bad First Date

-   Bumble's stock dropped 17% after its AI-powered turnaround failed to stop users from fleeing to Hinge. Apparently AI can't fix a broken dating app.

-   Why it matters: Slapping "AI-powered" on a declining product doesn't automatically make it good. Revolutionary concept, we know.

* * * * *

### 📌 Pinterest's AI Success Still Isn't Enough

-   Pinterest's AI ad suite is working great, but investors wanted *more* great, so the stock tanked anyway. Welcome to 2025, where success is failure.

-   Why it matters: In today's market, being profitable with AI isn't enough --- you need to be *obscenely* profitable with AI.

* * * * *

### 🏡 Airbnb CEO Delivers Rare Dose of Sanity

-   Brian Chesky said AI chatbots aren't the "new Google" yet, while quietly using AI to replace 15% of customer service humans. At least he's honest about the hype.

-   Why it matters: Finally, a CEO who understands that using the same AI as everyone else doesn't create a moat. Shocking insight.

### 🐦 Google's AI Actually Helps Save the Planet

-   Why it matters: Proof that AI can do more than generate fake LinkedIn posts. Sometimes it can actually make the world better.
