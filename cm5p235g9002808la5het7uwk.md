## ChatGPT Prompts for Research Writing

### Identify a research topic

When struggling to identify a research topic in the field of research writing, ChatGPT can be of great help.

```plaintext

My interest lies in the impact of digital tools on research writing.

Give me 5 potential research topics that I can explore.
```

Research questions are crucial for any research paper.

ChatGPT is an excellent tool to help you develop a research question.

