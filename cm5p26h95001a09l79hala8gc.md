### Brainstorm movie ideas

Whether you're suffering from writer's block or just need a fresh perspective, ChatGPT can be your creative partner in screenplay writing.

Simply provide the AI with the basic details of your story – the genre, setting, characters, or any specific scenes you'd like to include.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

