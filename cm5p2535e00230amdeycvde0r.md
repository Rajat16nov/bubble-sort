## ChatGPT Prompts for Study Schedule

### Identify primary subjects or topics of study

ChatGPT can be used to determine the primary subjects or topics of study for a specific study schedule.

This may include identifying the main topics or areas that require the most focus, planning the study schedule based on the complexity or weightage of the subjects, and suggesting the optimal amount of time to spend on each topic.

For example, you could ask ChatGPT to help organize a study schedule for a student preparing for a specific examination.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Help me build a study schedule for a student who is preparing for the upcoming final exams.

The subjects include English, Mathematics, History, Science, and Arts.

Consider the complexity of each subject and suggest how much time should be allocated for each.
```
