### Provide guidance on T-shirt design dimensions and sizing

When creating a T-shirt design, it is crucial to consider the dimensions and sizing.

The standard print size for t-shirts is typically 11 inches by 17 inches, which suits most men's and unisex sizes.

However, for women's or children's sizes, you may need to scale down the design to fit the smaller garment size.

Remember to maintain your design's aspect ratio when resizing to avoid distortion.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Also, provide tips on how to scale down designs for smaller sizes without losing the design's integrity.
```
