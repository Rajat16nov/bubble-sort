### Identify potential product categories for online selling

With the extensive growth of e-commerce, finding the right product to sell online can be a challenge.

ChatGPT can assist you in identifying potential product categories for online selling based on market trends, consumer preferences, and profitability.

Just provide ChatGPT with information regarding your target market, and it will suggest suitable product categories.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

