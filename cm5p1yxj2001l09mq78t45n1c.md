## ChatGPT Prompts for Tailoring Resume

### Identify key skills and qualifications for the targeted job

In order to make your resume stand out, it's essential to highlight relevant skills and qualifications for the job you're targeting.

Ask ChatGPT to analyze the job description and identify key skills and qualifications required for the job.

For instance, you can provide a job description for the role you're applying for, and ChatGPT will extract necessary skills and qualifications.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a career counselor and analyze this job description for a software engineer position at a tech company.

Identify and list down the key skills and qualifications required for this role.
```
