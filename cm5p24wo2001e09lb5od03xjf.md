## ChatGPT Prompts for Resume Bullet Points

### Highlight key professional achievements

Incorporate your key professional achievements into your resume with the help of ChatGPT.

You can ask it to identify the most impactful achievements from a list, or to help you phrase your accomplishments in a concise and compelling way.

For example, you can provide ChatGPT with details of your work experience and ask it to help articulate these into powerful resume bullet points.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced resume writer.

I am a software engineer with 5 years of experience, having worked on several complex projects, improving system efficiency and leading a small team.

Help me highlight my key professional achievements in bullet points for my resume.
```
