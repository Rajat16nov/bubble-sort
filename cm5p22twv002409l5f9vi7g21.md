Affiliate marketing is a performance-based marketing strategy where a business rewards affiliates for each customer brought by their marketing efforts.

ChatGPT can give you an overview of affiliate marketing, cover key terms like affiliate link, commission, and conversion rate, and describe the benefits and challenges.

You can also ask it to suggest strategies for starting and succeeding in affiliate marketing.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Explain key terms, benefits, challenges, and provide some strategies for getting started and achieving success in affiliate marketing.
```
