To create effective technical content, understanding your audience is key.

ChatGPT can help you in defining the audience profile, mapping out their needs, and outlining the level of technical knowledge they may possess.

You can provide a brief description of your target audience to ChatGPT and it will help you analyze their technical understanding and needs.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Our target audience is primarily non-technical business owners who are keen to integrate our software into their workflow.

Help me understand their needs, their level of technical understanding, and how to approach writing for them.
```
