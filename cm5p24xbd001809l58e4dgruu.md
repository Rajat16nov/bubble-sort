## ChatGPT Prompts for Resume Optimization

### Identify key skills and achievements to highlight

A resume needs to stand out in a pile and ChatGPT can help you do just that.

By providing your current resume or a list of your experiences, ChatGPT can identify key skills and achievements that you should highlight.

These may be based on your job industry, role, or specific skills.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Identify the key skills and achievements that I should highlight on my resume to make it more appealing to potential employers.
```

