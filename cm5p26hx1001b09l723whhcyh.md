### Provide information on the impact of inflation on savings and investments.

Inflation erodes the purchasing power of money over time, which can have a significant impact on your savings and investments.

If your savings and investments are not growing at a rate that surpasses inflation, you are essentially losing money in real terms.

For example, if you have money in a savings account that is earning a 1% return, but inflation is running at 2%, you are effectively losing 1% of your savings' purchasing power each year.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an experienced financial advisor, explain how inflation impacts savings and investments.

Include strategies for mitigating this impact and ensuring that one's savings and investments keep pace with or exceed the rate of inflation.
```
