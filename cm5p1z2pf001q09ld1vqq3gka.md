### Understand and apply the concept of keyword density

Keyword density is a vital aspect of SEO content writing.

It refers to the number of times a keyword appears in a piece of content as a percentage of the total word count.

Understanding and applying the correct keyword density can boost your SEO efforts by helping search engines understand your content's relevancy to specific search queries.

ChatGPT can assist in calculating keyword density or suggesting an optimal keyword density range for your content.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an SEO content writer, I need to understand and apply the concept of keyword density.

I have written a 500-word article targeting the keyword digital marketing strategies.

This keyword appears 10 times in my article.

Calculate the keyword density and suggest if I need to increase or decrease it for optimal SEO.
```
