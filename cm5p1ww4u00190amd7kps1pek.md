### Guide on setting up a TikTok Business Account

Setting up a TikTok Business Account can help your brand reach a wider audience.

First, download the TikTok app and create a personal account.

Then, navigate to the Settings menu and click Manage my account, where you'll find the option to Switch to Pro Account and choose Business.

Finally, complete your business profile including details like your business category and contact information.
