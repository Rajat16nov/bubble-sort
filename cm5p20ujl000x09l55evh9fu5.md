### Identify the purpose of the room

When it comes to interior design, it's essential to understand the function of each room to create a space that serves its intended purpose effectively.

ChatGPT can help you identify the purpose of a room based on the furniture, layout, and other design elements present in it.

```plaintext

What would be the primary function of this room?
```
