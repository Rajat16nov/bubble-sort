## ChatGPT Prompts for Facebook Ads

### Identify target audience for Facebook Ads

ChatGPT can help you identify your target audience for Facebook Ads, based on the nature of your business, product, or service.

Provide the AI with the details of your business or product, its benefits, and its potential consumers, and it will suggest a demographic that is likely to be interested in what you have to offer.

For example, you can ask ChatGPT to define the target audience for a new fitness app aimed at young adults.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

My business is launching a new fitness app specifically designed for young adults who are interested in maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Who should be the primary target audience for our Facebook Ads campaign?
```
