## ChatGPT Prompts for UPSC Preparation

### Understand the UPSC syllabus and exam pattern

The UPSC exam, a highly competitive government service exam in India, has a complex syllabus and exam pattern.

ChatGPT can help you to understand the various topics and subjects covered in the syllabus, as well as the pattern of questions asked in the exam.

Whether you want a breakdown of the Civil Services Preliminary Exam, the Main Exam, or the optional subjects, ChatGPT can provide a detailed understanding.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an expert on UPSC preparation, provide a detailed breakdown of the UPSC syllabus and exam pattern for the Civil Services Examination.

Include information about the Preliminary Exam, the Main Exam, and optional subjects.
```

### Develop a study plan for UPSC preparation

ChatGPT can develop a customized study plan for UPSC preparation by considering various factors like syllabus weightage, difficulty level, and personal strengths and weaknesses.

Just provide your availability, preferred study hours, and areas of difficulty, and ChatGPT will formulate a structured study plan.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

I find it challenging to grasp Economic concepts.

I also need to improve my General Studies.
```

### Learn to solve CSAT (Civil Services Aptitude Test) problems

If you're preparing for the UPSC CSAT, ChatGPT can be a valuable resource.

You can input various problem types, like comprehension, decision-making, logical reasoning, and general mental ability problems and ask the AI to solve them or provide step-by-step solutions.

For example, you can use ChatGPT to understand how to approach a comprehension problem, identify the context, and choose the best answer.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a UPSC CSAT tutor and provide a step-by-step solution to this comprehension problem:

The sun rises in the east and sets in the west.

This statement implies that the sun moves around the earth.

However, it is the earth that rotates on its axis, which gives the impression of the sun moving across the sky.

Question: What does the above passage primarily discuss?

a) The movement of the sun

b) The rotation of the earth

c) The illusion of the sun's movement

d) The sun's axis
```
