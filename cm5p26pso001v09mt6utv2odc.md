## ChatGPT Prompts for Document Analysis

### Identify the purpose of the document

Input a document's content into ChatGPT and ask it to identify the primary purpose or objective of the document.

Whether it's a legal contract, research paper, or business proposal, ChatGPT can help elucidate the main goal or point of the document.

For instance, you can feed ChatGPT with a corporate report and ask it to identify the report's main objectives.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced document analyst and identify the main objective of this document.

Here is the document content to be analyzed:
```

### Analyze the structure of the document

Understanding the structure of a document can be a complex task, but ChatGPT can assist you in breaking it down into manageable sections.

Just provide ChatGPT with the document you want to analyze, and it will scan through it to identify its structural elements such as headings, sub-headings, paragraphs, footnotes, etc.

This can be particularly useful for long documents or academic texts where structure is critical.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an experienced document analyst, review and break down the structure of the following text document.

Identify the main headings, sub-headings, paragraphs, and any other notable structural elements.

Here is the document to be analyzed:
```
