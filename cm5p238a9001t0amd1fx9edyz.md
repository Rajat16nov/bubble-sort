## ChatGPT Prompts for Academic Research Writing

### Identify a research topic or question

Research is an integral part of academic writing.

It is always challenging to find a unique and engaging topic.

ChatGPT can assist you in identifying a research topic or formulating a research question based on your subject of interest.

Just provide a broad area of study, and it will help you narrow it down to a specific research question or topic.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced academic research advisor.

I am majoring in environmental studies and I need to identify a unique research topic or formulate a research question for my dissertation.

Please help me brainstorm some potential topics or questions.
```

### Perform a literature review

Academic research writing can be a daunting task, but with ChatGPT, you can ease the process.

ChatGPT can review and summarize a wide range of academic literature, identify key themes and gaps, and analyze the current knowledge in the field.

Simply provide the research topic and specify the scope of the literature to be reviewed.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a researcher performing a literature review on the impact of climate change on global food security.

Summarize the key findings from the most relevant research articles and identify potential gaps for further study.
```

ChatGPT can help you clarify and define your research objectives for academic writing.

By providing a summary of your intended research topic, ChatGPT can generate a list of specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound objectives.

For instance, you can ask ChatGPT to define objectives for a research paper on the impact of global warming on polar bear populations.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

