### Write formulas for various calculations

Google Sheets offers a wide range of formulas to calculate and analyze your data effectively.

For instance, to calculate the sum of a column, you could use =SUM(A1:A10), where A1:A10 represents the range of cells in column A.

To find the average, =AVERAGE(B1:B10) would give you the average of the values in cells B1 to B10.

For more complex calculations, like a percentage increase, you might use a formula like =(C2-C1)/C1, where C2 is the end value and C1 is the start value.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a proficient Google Sheets user and provide formulas for different calculations.

I need to calculate total sales, average sales, and the percentage increase in sales from January to February.

Here is the sales data to be processed:
```
