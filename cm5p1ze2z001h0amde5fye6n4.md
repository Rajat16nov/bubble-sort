## ChatGPT Prompts for Resume

### Review and analyze current resume

In a competitive job market, your resume needs to stand out.

ChatGPT can review your existing resume and provide insights on how to make it more appealing.

It can check for common mistakes, suggest improvements and even recommend specific phrasing or action verbs to better highlight your achievements.

For example, you can ask ChatGPT to review your resume for conciseness, clarity, and relevance.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced HR professional and provide a detailed analysis of the following resume.

Check for common mistakes, suggest improvements, and ensure the resume highlights the candidate's skills and experiences effectively.

Here is the resume to be reviewed:
```
