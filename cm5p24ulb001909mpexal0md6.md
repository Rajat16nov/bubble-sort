## ChatGPT Prompts for Resume Summary

### Write a concise professional title

Crafting a professional title for your resume can be a daunting task.

However, with ChatGPT, you can simply provide details about your skills, experiences, and the job you're aiming for.

ChatGPT will then use this information to generate a concise and catchy professional title that aligns with your career objectives.

For example, you can direct ChatGPT to come up with a title that fits your career profile in the tech industry.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a career coach to help me come up with a professional title for my resume.

I am a software engineer with 5 years of experience, specializing in frontend development with a focus on React and Vue.js.

I am aiming for a senior position at a tech firm focusing on e-commerce solutions.
```
