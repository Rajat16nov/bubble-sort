### Provide tips on the use of alliteration and assonance

Alliteration and assonance are essential tools in poetry to create rhythm, mood, and visual imagery.

Use alliteration - the repetition of initial consonant sounds, to add a musical element to your poem.

Assonance, the repetition of vowel sounds, can provide a soft or harsh tone, depending on the chosen vowel.

Start by identifying words that convey the emotions and imagery you want, then find matching sounds to create alliteration or assonance.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an experienced poet, provide some tips on the effective use of alliteration and assonance in a poem.

Suppose the poem's theme revolves around the tranquility and serenity of a moonlit night.
```
