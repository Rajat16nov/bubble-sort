### Understand the concept of compound interest

Compound interest is a financial term that is crucial to understand when managing or investing money.

It refers to the process where the interest you earn on a sum of money is added back to the original amount, and then that total sum earns interest, and so on.

ChatGPT can explain this concept in an easy-to-understand manner using practical examples.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```
