## ChatGPT Prompts for Lawyers

### Research legal precedents

Working through legal precedents can be challenging.

However, ChatGPT can assist in researching and summarizing these cases.

It can provide a brief overview of significant decisions related to a specific legal topic, aiding lawyers in their case preparation.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an intelligent legal assistant, research and summarize key legal precedents related to personal injury law.

Consider both landmark cases and recent decisions to provide a comprehensive understanding of the legal landscape in this area.
```
