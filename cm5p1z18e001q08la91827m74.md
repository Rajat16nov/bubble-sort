### Understand and explain the concept of stock splits and reverse stock splits

In the world of stock trading, stock splits and reverse stock splits play a significant role.

A stock split is when a company divides its existing shares into multiple shares to boost liquidity.

Although the number of shares increases, the total value remains the same, making the shares more affordable to small investors.

A reverse stock split, on the other hand, merges multiple shares into a single share, effectively increasing the stock price.

It is often used by companies to meet the minimum share price requirements to remain listed on a stock exchange.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>
