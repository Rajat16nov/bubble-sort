## ChatGPT Prompts for Human Writing

### Generate brainstorming prompts for a writing session

For a writing session, one may need to explore various aspects of 'Human Writing'.

ChatGPT can aid in brainstorming by suggesting writing prompts based on this theme.

You can ask ChatGPT to explore the evolution of writing, the cultural impacts of writing, or the psychological aspects of writing.

```plaintext

```

### Suggest ways to build suspense in a story

To create suspense in a story, you can introduce uncertainty or unpredictability about what's going to happen next.

You could use foreshadowing, where you give a hint or warning of a future event.

Dramatic irony, where the audience knows something that the characters do not, can also build suspense.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As an experienced author, share advice on how to build suspense in a story.

Discuss the impact of foreshadowing, dramatic irony, and the element of uncertainty.

Provide examples of how these techniques can be used effectively in writing.
```
