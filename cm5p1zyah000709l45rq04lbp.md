## ChatGPT Prompts for Networking

### Understand the basics of networking

Networking is the practice of connecting computers and other devices together to share resources.

The basic components of networking include hardware (such as routers, switches, and servers), protocols (like TCP/IP), and the network interface (which could be wired or wireless).

ChatGPT can help explain these concepts or even guide a beginner through setting up a simple network.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a seasoned networking professional and explain the basics of networking to a beginner.

Please cover topics such as hardware, protocols, and network interfaces.
```
