## ChatGPT Prompts for Interview Questions

### Prepare common interview questions for a job applicant

ChatGPT can assist HR managers and hiring teams by generating standard interview questions tailored for specific job roles.

Just provide the job title and primary responsibilities, and ChatGPT will create relevant questions to assess the candidate's competence, experience, and skills.

For instance, you can ask ChatGPT to generate questions for a software developer interview.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as an experienced HR manager and prepare common interview questions for a job applicant applying for the position of a software developer.

The main responsibilities of the role include coding, debugging, performing software testing, and creating technical documentation.
```
