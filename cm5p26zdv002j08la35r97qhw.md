## ChatGPT Prompts for Analyzing Data

### Understand the goals and objectives of data analysis

Data analysis involves inspecting, cleaning, transforming, and modeling data to discover useful information.

It aims to draw conclusions that aid in decision-making.

The primary objectives are to uncover trends, identify patterns, and infer meaningful insights from raw data to help organizations make informed decisions.

For instance, you can ask ChatGPT to summarize the key takeaways from a large dataset.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

Evaluate the following dataset and provide a summary of key findings and trends.

Here is the dataset to be analyzed:
```
