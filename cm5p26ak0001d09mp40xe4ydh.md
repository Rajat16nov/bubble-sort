### Understand the concept of compound interest

Compound interest is the concept where interest earned accumulates and earns more interest over time.

In investing, it's a powerful tool as the interest on your initial investment grows exponentially, thus increasing your overall returns.

You can ask ChatGPT to calculate compound interest or explain it further with real-life examples.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

```

### Learn about dollar-cost averaging

Dollar-cost averaging (DCA) is a common investment strategy where you invest a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, regardless of the asset price.

This strategy reduces the impact of volatility and minimizes the risk of investing a large amount in a single investment at the wrong time.

For example, you could ask ChatGPT to simulate different DCA scenarios to understand how it impacts your investment portfolio.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
Act as a financial advisor and explain the concept of dollar-cost averaging (DCA).

Also, illustrate how DCA works by providing a few examples.
```
