Achieving your fitness goals requires a structured and well-planned workout schedule.

ChatGPT can assist in creating one tailored to your specific needs.

Provide ChatGPT with your fitness objectives, the frequency of your workouts, and the types of exercises you enjoy.

It will then design a suitable workout plan for you.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext

I am a beginner, my goal is to lose weight and build muscle, I can go to the gym five days a week, and I enjoy strength training and cardio.

Create a workout schedule tailored to my needs.
```
