### Understand the difference between formal and informal German

When learning German, it's crucial to understand the differences between formal and informal language.

Formal German is used in professional, academic, or formal social settings, while informal German is used among friends, family, and peers.

For instance, the use of 'Sie' and 'Du' represents formal and informal 'you' respectively.

<strong>ChatGPT Prompt:</strong>

```plaintext
As a language tutor, explain the differences between formal and informal German, providing examples and contexts where each would be appropriately used.
```
